How To Request Spotify Data
  1. Open spotify.com/us/account/privacy.
  2. In Download your data, tick Extended streaming history (required for this dashboard). You can also keep Account data ticked.
  3. Submit the request, then confirm it from the Spotify email.
  4. Wait a couple of hours for Spotify to send a download link.
  5. Download the ZIP and upload it here.
